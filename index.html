Who I am

IT Systems Engineer & Aspiring Developer

I am an Information Technology Professional & Aspiring Developer based in Glasgow, Scotland


For the last 10 years I have been working at a Managed Service Provider supporting over 100 different client infrastructures and many different technologies. Ranging from traditional on-premise server infrastructure to full cloud infrastructure utilising Microsoft 365 and Microsoft Azure. I started as an apprentice and now I am responsible for the business Internal Systems and Processes.


I have always had an interest and passion for coding and development, I would always find myself coming back to learning development. I am not making the leap to change my career path into my passion for Software Development. I am self-taught and always looking to improve.
